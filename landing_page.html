The Home of Chasing Sunsets

Kenya is one of the world's magnificent wildlife destinations! Its parks, reserves and private conservancies are home to some of the highest and most diverse populations of wildlife on the planet. Traveling across vast landscapes bathed in soft morning light, your African dreams unfold before your eyes.

Travel Destinations In Kenya

Diani

Diani is famous for its white sand beaches, blue ocean and surfing

Masaai Mara

World famous for hosting the epic Great Migration, the Masai Mara welcomes 1,5 million wildebeests onto its sprawling savannahs each July through October. The Masai Mara National Reserve and conservancies are brimming with life and offer safari travellers a wide variety of activities to choose from.

Mt Kenya

Due to its unique ecosystems which change the higher you go up the mountain, Mount Kenya has been designated a UNESCO World Heritage Site and Biosphere Reserve. Many climbers, hikers, trekkers and adventure enthusiasts challenge themselves by ascending the mountain each year.

Kenyan Tea Plantations

Tea plantations are unique travel destinations that offer a combination of scenic beauty, cultural experiences, and historical significance.
